### Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist for College Students

#### DO's (Best Practices)

1. **DO Think Before You Post**
   - Think how your post might be viewed by others
   - Ask yourself: "Would I want my professors/future employers to see this?"
   - Check your contents for spellings, grammars, and tone before you share it

2. **DO Use Privacy Settings Wisely**
   - Make your profiles private if possiblv
   - Check your privacys settings regularly
   - Keep your personal information private
   - Control who sees your posts and stories

3. **DO Post Positively and Constructively**
   - Share content that is informative
   - Support your friends
   - Join in meaningful discussions
   - Use your platforms to spread awareness about things that matter

4. **DO Check Your Facts Before Sharing Them**
   - Check your sources before you share
   - Watch out for fake news
   - Check your sources before you share something you saw going viral
   - Do not spread rumors or false information

5. **DO Keep Your Professional Life Professional**
   - Keep your personal profiles separate from your professional profiles
   - Do not accept friend requests from professors/colleagues unless you really want to
   - Use LinkedIn

---

## DON'Ts (Avoid These)

1. **DON'T Share Sensitive Personal Information**
   - Do not post your home address, phone number, or Aadhaar card information.
   - Avoid posting your location in real-time (e.g., vacation posts, check-ins).
   - Do not post your passwords or OTPs, even as a joke.
   - Do not post your daily routine or schedule.

2. **DON'T Engage in Cyberbullying or Harassment**
   - Do not post hurtful or offending posts about your classmates or other people.
   - Do not post pictures of other people that can embarrass or offend them.
   - Do not engage in gossiping or rumor-spreading.
   - Do not cyberbully or harass anyone.

3. **DON'T Post Inappropriate Content**
   - Do not post anything that can be offensive, discriminatory, or hateful.
   - Do not post explicit pictures or videos.
   - Do not post while you are under the influence of drugs or alcohol.
   - Do not post your political opinions or views, as they might be controversial.

4. **DON'T Overshare or Post Constantly**
   - Do not posts all the details of your personal life.
   - Do not spam your followers by posting multiple posts in a short time.
   - Do not rant about specific people or topics.
