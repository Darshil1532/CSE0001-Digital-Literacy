# Stay Safe Online - Prevention Checklist for College Students

## 🔒 Essential Online Safety Tips

### 1. **Strong Passwords & Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**
- [ ] Use unique passwords for all accounts (minimum 12 characters, mixed with letters, numbers, and symbols)
- [ ] Enable 2FA on all important accounts (Google, bank, social media, etc.)

### 2. **Recognize & Avoid Phishing Scams**
- [ ] Be cautious with sender emails before clicking on any link or attachment
- [ ] Do not click on links such as "your account is blocked" or "urgent action required"

### 3. **Social Media Privacy Settings**
- [ ] Make your social media profiles private (only visible to your friends)
- [ ] Do not disclose your phone number, address, college, etc., on your social profiles

### 4. **Safe Browsing Habits**
- [ ] Keep your operating system and all apps updated with the latest patches and security fixes
- [ ] Install an antivirus and turn on the firewall on your computer

### 5. **Backup Important Data**
- [ ] Regularly save your phone and laptop data on cloud storage (Google Drive, OneDrive, etc.)

### 6. **Think Before You Share**
- [ ] Never share your OTP, CVV, or any bank details with anyone
- [ ] Do not publicly declare your vacation plans on social media,

## 💰 Financial & UPI Safety Tips

### 7. **UPI & Digital Payment Safety**
- [ ] Never share UPI PINs with anyone - not even "customr cares" or "any bank official"
- [ ] Verify UPI ID twices before sending moneys - transaction are instant and irreversible for any one

### 8. **Banking & Card Security**
- [ ] Enable transactions alert via SMS/emails for all accountss
- [ ] Set daily transaction limits on UPIs and card
